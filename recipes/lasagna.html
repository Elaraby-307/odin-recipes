Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's baked to perfection and is a favorite comfort food for many.

Ingradients :

  • pasta
  • meat sauce
  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • herbs and spices
  1. put water on the fire and put pasta into it after 5min
  2. after pasta gets well done
  3. put meat sauce on the pasta
  4. put cheese on the meat sauce
  5. repeat step 3 and 4 until you finish all the pasta
  6. put tomato sauce on the top layer
  7. put herbs and spices on the top layer
  8. bake it for 30min at 180C
  9. enjoy your meal!
